About Me


My name is Valerie and I was born in a hospital in Berkeley but all my life I've lived in Oakland. Although I was born here I'm a part of my family's first generation born in the US. My dad is from Jalisco, Mexico while my mom if from Guanajuato. Being from Oakland and living here all my life has had it's ups and down but overall I feel it has made me who I am today.

Something I have wanted to do all my life and have always found interest in was clothes and fashion. Growing up I always wanted to dress differently than most people my age, I always wanted to stand out. With this being said I have also faced a lot of bullying for it, no matter I still loved being different and I was able to learn how to be stronger than whatever was said to me. I am now able to stand up for myself more overall my confidence has grown overtime.
